The advent of the internet and the subsequent 
explosion of mobile technology have completely 
redefined the landscape of human communication.
In the past, connecting with someone meant
writing letters, making scheduled phone calls,
or meeting in person. Today, instant messaging, 
video calls, and social media platforms have 
made distance nearly irrelevant, allowing for
real-time interaction across continents. This
shift has facilitated faster communication, 
enabling businesses to operate globally and 
helping people maintain relationships despite
physical separation. The ability to share 
experiences instantly, whether through text,
 photos, or live video, keeps individuals 
 connected in ways previously unimaginable.
